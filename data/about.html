About Immrama

If you wish to perform Immrama, you current have three different options:

  1. Install it on a computer you control and use it from there.
  2. Install it on a Raspberry Pi and have users connect to it as if it were a network login page.
  3. This website will turn into a a platform for ensembles that wish to perform the piece.

If you wish to discuss what is best for your group and your users, please get in touch.
